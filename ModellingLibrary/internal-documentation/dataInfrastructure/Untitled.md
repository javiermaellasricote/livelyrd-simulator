## Trading spoons for forks

#### Objects:

* Two players, each with:

  * Two resources (R): spoons, forks
  * Two actions: get rid of spoons and get forks; and vice-versa
  * One objective: 

  $$
  \max(R_{T,1,player1} \cdot R_{T,2,player1} + R_{T,1,player2} \cdot R_{T,2,player2})
  $$
